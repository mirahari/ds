TIME SERIES ANALYSIS¶


INTRODUCTION¶

The dataset used is Average (Mean) Monthly Nominal Earnings Per Employee By Sex, Quarterly from Data.gov.sg.

Average (Mean) Monthly Earnings refers to all remuneration received before deduction of the employee Central Provident Fund (CPF) contributions and personal income tax. It comprises basic wages, overtime pay, commissions, allowances and bonuses but exclude employer CPF contributions.
